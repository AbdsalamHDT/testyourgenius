Our Mission

To democratize access to genius-level thinking by providing cutting-edge cognitive assessment tools and transformative mentorship experiences. We bridge the gap between potential and achievement, helping individuals discover their unique intellectual strengths and develop them through proven methodologies derived from history's most brilliant minds.

The Genesis of Genius

Test Your Genius was born from a simple yet profound observation: traditional intelligence testing tells you where you stand, but not how to reach your peak potential. Our founders, cognitive scientists and education innovators, recognized that true genius isn't just about raw intelligence—it's about thinking patterns, learning strategies, and mental frameworks.

By studying the documented approaches of history's greatest minds—from Einstein's thought experiments to Da Vinci's systematic curiosity—we've created a unique platform that not only measures cognitive ability but actively develops it through personalized mentorship experiences.
