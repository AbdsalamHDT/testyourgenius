Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Overview

TestYourGenius is designed with privacy at its core. Your assessment data, results, and reflections are stored entirely on your device using your browser's local storage. We do not have a server that collects, stores, or processes your personal psychological data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your assessment answers
  • Your cognitive profile or archetype results
  • Your reflection journal entries
  • Any data stored in your browser's localStorage

Data We May Collect

If you create an account, we store minimal information needed for authentication:

  • Name and email address — used for login and communication
  • Payment information — processed through secure third-party providers; we do not store card numbers

We may also collect anonymous usage analytics (page views, general interaction patterns) to improve the product. This data cannot be linked to your assessment results.

Local Storage

All assessment data is stored in your browser's localStorage under keys prefixed with tyg_. This data never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data or using the retake assessment option.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Hosting — GitHub Pages for static site hosting
  • Analytics — anonymous, aggregated analytics only
  • Payment processing — industry-standard secure payment providers

Your Rights

Since your psychological data stays on your device, you have full control over it. You can view, export, or delete your data at any time by accessing your browser's developer tools or clearing site data.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us through the website.
