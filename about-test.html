About the TYG Assessment

A multi-dimensional self-discovery tool that maps how you think, what you need, and how you respond under pressure — giving you a rich, personal inner map.

ⓘ This is not a medical diagnosis. It is a self-awareness tool designed for personal growth and reflection.

What It Measures

🧠

Core Mind Style

Maps your dominant cognitive archetype — how you naturally process information, make decisions, and view the world. Identifies which of six core "islands" shape your thinking.

🎯

Needs Map

Measures your psychological needs across three dimensions: Autonomy, Competence, and Relatedness. Reveals what drives you and where you may feel unfulfilled.

🔗

Connection Style

Explores how you form and maintain close relationships. Assesses attachment patterns — your comfort with intimacy, independence, and emotional vulnerability.

⚡

Stress & Coping

Identifies your stress-response modes and coping mechanisms under pressure. Maps both protective strategies and your underlying Healthy Adult resilience.

Your Archetype Islands

The 24 archetypes are organized into 6 core islands — each representing a dominant way of thinking and relating.

Each island contains four archetypes shaped by your thinking style, emotional needs, and stress patterns.

Why It's Different

01

Multi-Dimensional

Most assessments give you one label. TYG maps four interconnected dimensions simultaneously — cognition, needs, attachment, and stress — for a complete picture.

02

Emergent Archetypes

Your archetype isn't assigned from a preset list. It emerges from the unique combination of your scores — meaning your result is genuinely yours, not a box to fit into.

03

Designed as a Journey

The assessment is structured to be a reflective experience, not a rushed quiz. Each module builds on the last, guiding you through layers of self-understanding.

Designed for Clarity, Not Labels.

  • Your results are processed entirely in your browser — nothing is sent to a server.
  • No data is collected, stored externally, or shared with anyone.
  • This tool does not diagnose, label, or categorise you clinically.
  • Results are nuanced and personal — they are a starting point for reflection, not a final verdict.
  • Built with mental-health-first principles: empowerment over judgement.
